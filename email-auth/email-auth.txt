   ptype                   Definition                                                        Description
   body   [RFC-ietf-appsawg-rfc7001bis-10] section 2.3 The property being reported was found in the body of the message.
   header [RFC-ietf-appsawg-rfc7001bis-10] section 2.3 The property being reported was found in a header field of the message.
   policy [RFC-ietf-appsawg-rfc7001bis-10] section 2.3 The property being reported relates to a locally-defined policy.
   smtp   [RFC-ietf-appsawg-rfc7001bis-10] section 2.3 The property being reported is a parameter to an SMTP command used to relay the message.
