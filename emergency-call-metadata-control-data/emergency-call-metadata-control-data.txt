               view)

               separate from backup view
   lane        Used by systems to identify road lane and/or monitor vehicle's position within lane  [RFC-ietf-ecrit-car-crash-23] IESG
   interior    Shows the interior (e.g., driver)                                                    [RFC-ietf-ecrit-car-crash-23] IESG
   night-front Night-vision view of what is in front of the vehicle                                 [RFC-ietf-ecrit-car-crash-23] IESG

   night-left  Night-vision view of what is to the left of the vehicle                              [RFC-ietf-ecrit-car-crash-23] IESG
   night-right Night-vision view of what is to the right of the vehicle                             [RFC-ietf-ecrit-car-crash-23] IESG
